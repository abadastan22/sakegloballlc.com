About us

Professional service delivery with accountability and purpose.

SAKE Global LLC is a Florida-based business solutions company supporting government, institutional, and commercial clients.

Who We Are

SAKE Global LLC provides practical, performance-driven support across facility services, procurement coordination, administrative operations, logistics, project management, and consulting. Our model is built around disciplined execution, responsive communication, and clear documentation.

We work with agencies, prime contractors, facility owners, and private organizations that need a dependable partner capable of aligning service delivery with contract requirements and operational priorities.

Our Operating Principles

  • Integrity in every client engagement
  • Safety-first field operations
  • Compliance with scope, schedule, and documentation requirements
  • Responsive management and escalation control
  • Continuous improvement through measurable service standards

Mission

To deliver reliable, compliant, and cost-effective business and facility support solutions that help clients meet mission-critical objectives.

Vision

To become a trusted small business partner for public agencies, prime contractors, and commercial organizations across the United States.

Values

Accountability, professionalism, responsiveness, integrity, safety, and client-focused execution.
